How much does it cost to enroll into DiSCOVER?

The price of the program, which is heavily subsidized by the Mon Sheong Foundation, is $2,100. The price will cover most costs of the summer program leading up to the trip, and all costs for the trip (round-trip airfare, in-land transportation and accommodation, and meals). Please note that this price does not include travel insurance, tips, or China visa.

What is the cancellation/refund policy for DiSCOVER?

There is a strict, no-refund policy for the DiSCOVER program. Once an applicant has been accepted, he or she will have one week to accept or decline, and to make the full payment. Once payment has been received, we unfortunately cannot make a refund. This policy is in place because many of the expenses we incur are non-refundable. These costs include a sizeable deposit to the travel agency for flight and accommodation arrangements, a deposit for the DiSCOVER leadership retreat location, and the purchase of program materials.

How can I join DiSCOVER as a volunteer/executive?

For any inquiries about joining our team, please email discover@monsheong.org. There are also volunteer opportunities with the Mon Sheong Youth Group (MSYG), which organizes events such as socials, speaker events, performances for seniors, and fundraisers. Send an email to msyg@monsheong.org to express your interest and we will get back to you shortly on how you can be involved! You can also request to be put on our mailing list to keep up-to-date with events.

What are the requirements to apply?

Applicants must:
  1. Be between the age of 19 to 30 as of April 29, 2017.
  2. Own a Canadian or Chinese passport, valid until at least February 2018.
  3. Be of Chinese origin and/or have a strong interest in the Chinese culture.
  4. Have interest and extensive experience in community involvement and volunteering.
  5. Be proficient in English. Note: Mandarin lessons are included in the DiSCOVER 2017 program.
  6. Be able to travel from July 24, 2017 - August 7, 2017.
  7. Attend and participate in events before and after the trip e.g. Leadership Retreat. Programs will be scheduled on weekends and weekday evenings.


What is the application process?

There are two stages to the selection process:
  1. Application
    • Personal/Academic/Supporting Information
    • Volunteer Experience
    • Application Questions
    • References
    • Resume
  2. In-person panel interview (March 18 & 25 weekends)
    • Shortlisted candidates will be contacted for interviews.

I'm interested in the DiSCOVER program, but will be away at school during the school year. Will I still be able to participate?

DiSCOVER classes are scheduled to commence at the beginning of May following university exams. Workshops and lessons will be held on one day of the weekend. In order to gain the most from the program, you will be expected to make significant efforts to attend all classes.

What if I'm already fluent in Mandarin? Is the Mandarin course still mandatory?

It is optional. However, attending would give you a chance to meet and interact with the other trip participants; perhaps you can take on a mentor role in the class and help out your classmates!

Where will the classes and workshops be held?

Most of the classes and events will be held the Mon Sheong Scarborough Long Term Care Centre located at 2030 McNicoll Avenue, Scarborough, Ontario M1V 5P4.

My child is interested in the DiSCOVER Program. How can I stay informed about their activities while they are away in China?

Participants of DiSCOVER will maintain a blog on their experiences. Parents can follow this blog for updates from the group. Trip leaders will be equipped with cell phones and participants can also be reached at the hotels they will be staying at.

Will there be tour guides for the DiSCOVER participants in China?

Yes. There will be local tour guides to introduce participants to the various destinations, and to provide cultural, educational and historical insights.

What are the hotel accommodations?

All hotels in China are 4 or 5-star. There will be 2 participants per room of the same gender.

Will I have free time in China?

Yes. While there are many activities planned for trip participants, there will also be free time for exploring, shopping, sightseeing, and relaxation. Participants will be required to travel in small groups.

Will I be allowed to stay out late at night during our stay in China?

Participants will have free time after the planned activities, however, a 12 a.m. curfew will be in place for safety reasons.

Will I be able to extend my stay in China, make my own travel arrangements to a different country, or change my airline tickets from round-trip to one-way?

No. For safety reasons, and because travel arrangements will be made for the group as a whole, you will not be allowed to extend your stay, travel independently, or change your airline tickets from round-trip to one-way.
